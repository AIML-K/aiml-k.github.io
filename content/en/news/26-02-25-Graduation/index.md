Congratulations to our five newly minted graduates! This spring graduation is especially meaningful for AIML@K, as we celebrate Jaeheun Jung, our very first Ph.D. graduate, together with four new M.S. graduates: Yeajin Lee, Bosung Jung, Suhyun Bae, and Shuaizhuo Yuan. May the Force be with you as you embark on your next adventure.

<!--more-->

Jaeheun Jung now holds a Ph.D. degree in Mathematics, becoming the first Ph.D. graduate from AIML@K. 
This milestone means a great deal to our lab, marking not only Jaeheun’s personal achievement but also an important chapter in the growth of our research community.
Throughout his time with us, Jaeheun has helped shape the spirit of AIML@K through his persistence, intellectual curiosity, and steady commitment to research. 
His work has reflected the kind of careful thinking, mathematical depth, and creative problem-solving that we hope will continue to define our lab.
We are deeply grateful for the path he has helped open for future students. 
Although it is bittersweet to see him move on, this is also a moment of great pride. Thank you, Jaeheun — for your dedication, leadership, and the lasting mark you leave on AIML@K. 
We are excited to see the many contributions you will make in the years ahead.

----

We are grateful for the care and dedication she shared with us, and we will remember not only her academic contributions but also the warmth and kindness she brought to the lab community.
Thank you, Yeajin — we wish you every success and happiness in your next chapter.
